My name is Beth O’Loughlin and I would love to be involved with your special day be it a marriage or civil union.

I look forward to hearing your story and then assisting in crafting a special service especially for you. I have an event management background which can assist in ensuring your day goes smoothly. If required, I can help you with any aspect of your planning.

I am based in Parnell, Auckland and am available for services in Auckland City and environs.

Please email me and I will respond with availability and cost. I would love to meet with you and have some fun planning your special day!

About Me

Being appointed a marriage celebrant has fulfilled a life-long dream for me.

I moved to Auckland 15 years ago and fell in love with Parnell where I still live and am heavily involved in the community.

I work as an office manager for a busy professional practice in Newmarket. I hail from the South Island where I had my own events management business so know all about organising events!

I am from a large (and yes we were Catholic) family, am a mother of two adult children so appreciate the value of families and know all about family dynamics.

Being raised a Catholic, I know all about pomp and ceremony!

I have excellent written and verbal skills and hold a Certificate in Freelance Journalism from the New Zealand Institute of Business Studies.

I am presently in the process of completing my Certificate in Celebrant Studies at The Celebrant School. This training ensures you are in good hands.

My hobbies are keeping fit (I’m a gym junkie), reading, current affairs (and a newspaper junkie!) and politics.

What Next?

  • Let's meet and you can check me out - my place or a local cafe at a time to suit
  • This will help you decide if you are happy to proceed with me officiating on your big day
  • We will discuss the time, date and proposed structure of your ceremony
  • I will advise you of the legal requirements and ensure paperwork requirements are covered
  • I will give you options for vows and ceremony structure and format
  • We will discuss fees

Then...

  • Once you've given me the green light, I will write a unique ceremony incorporating your love story
  • I will provide you with a copy of the script in draft form and then tweak as required
  • On the day I will arrive half an hour before the ceremony and check everything is in order
  • And then enjoy your big day!

Fees

  • Provided on application - my fees are moderate.
